About Us

The San Joaquin Valley Lecture Series is a volunteer-driven non-profit committed to enriching our community through engaging, informative presentations. Our mission is to welcome renowned voices—both national and global—that inspire curiosity and spark meaningful conversation.

“Every year, I gift season tickets to my family—it’s the perfect way to share wisdom and discovery.”

Featured Speaker

Fossils & The Web of Life

February: Scott Sampson

Imagine unearthing a teenage Utahceratops—75 million years old and newly discovered! Join us at the Saroyan Theatre as Scott Sampson, a leading paleontologist and Research Curator at the Utah Museum of Natural History, shares insights from his fieldwork across Africa and beyond, revealing secrets of the Cretaceous period.
